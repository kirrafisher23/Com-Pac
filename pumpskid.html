Pump Skids

Com-Pac offers complete prepackaged pumping skids, designed in-house to meet your specifications. Available in materials like stainless steel, Kynar, PVDF, HDPE, and polypropylene, these systems feature peristaltic hose/tube or diaphragm pumps. Each unit comes pre-plumbed, prewired, and equipped with CPI BLOK control panels for seamless operation.
