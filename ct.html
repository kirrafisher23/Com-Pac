Collector Tanks and Filter Tanks

Explore our range of filter/surge tanks designed to efficiently trap and collect impurities from your water supply. These tanks, are a key component of our filtration systems. We offer multiple fiberglass molds, as well as a stainless steel options.

Collector/Surge Tanks

Collector and Surge Tanks are composite construction. Designed for In-Ground or Free Standing installations. All stub-outs are PVC and integrated into the tank structure. All tanks can be ordered with gravity valves, automatic level controls, and covers. Standard stub-outs include Main Drain, Surface Overflow, Suction, Fresh Water, and Tank Overflow.
